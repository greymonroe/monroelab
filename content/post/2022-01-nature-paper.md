Our landmark study published in *Nature* demonstrates that mutation bias in the *Arabidopsis thaliana* genome reflects natural selection. Deleterious mutations are less likely to occur in functionally important regions of the genome. This work was covered by NPR Science Friday, the Nature Podcast, and The Scientist.
